Religion Information
About Religions

Religions in the Country Game can provide various benefits:

  • Cultural identity and unity
  • Diplomatic relations with countries of the same faith
  • Access to special religious units
  • Potential bonuses to certain stats
  • Unique project opportunities
Religion Mechanics

Important mechanics to know:

  • Countries can have one official religion
  • Changing religions may have diplomatic consequences
  • Religious conflicts can arise between nations of different faiths
  • Some special forces units require specific religions
