Snake Game - How to Play

Game Controls:

Use the arrow keys to control the snake:

  • ↑ - Move Up
  • ↓ - Move Down
  • ← - Move Left
  • → - Move Right

Game Rules:

  1. Press the "Start Game" button to begin the game.
  2. Control the snake using the arrow keys.
  3. Eat the food (red circle) to grow longer and increase your score.
  4. Avoid hitting the walls or yourself - this will end the game!
  5. Try to achieve the highest score possible.

Tips:

  • Plan your moves ahead to avoid getting trapped.
  • Use the edges of the game area carefully.
  • The snake moves automatically in the direction you choose.
  • You cannot reverse direction directly (e.g., from right to left).
